Projects

1. Prosodic disambiguation of Korean information-seeking vs rhetorical questions

Using a production experiment, I am examining whether consistent prosodic cues exist in Korean to disambiguate string-identical polar questions that can be interpreted as information-seeking or rhetorical.

2. A semantic analysis of Korean -ullay: Subjects in egophoric constructions

Building on the claim that the Korean morpheme -ullay is an egophoric marker, I argued that subjects in egophoric constructions are not only limited to singular 1st and 2nd person pronouns, but can also be plural pronouns, collective nouns, and wh- words as long as a subset condition between the sentential subject and epistemic authority are met.
